About Atbash Cipher:

The Atbash cipher is a simple substitution cipher where each letter of the alphabet is mapped to its reverse. That is, 'A' becomes 'Z', 'B' becomes 'Y', and so on. This cipher is a monoalphabetic substitution cipher that transposes each letter of the plaintext with its counterpart from the end of the alphabet.
